Café & Hospitality

Coastal café rebrand

The challenge

A much-loved local café looked nothing like the experience inside — mismatched signage, an inconsistent feed, and no identity a customer could recognise.

What we did

Built a warm, confident brand identity, then rolled it across the menu, signage and a social grid with a clear, repeatable content style.

The result

A café that finally looks as good as it tastes — and a feed regulars actually share with friends.

1Cohesive identity, everywhere
3×More consistent posting
↑Shareable, on-brand content
Retail

Festive sales campaign

The challenge

A retail store wanted a strong festive season but had only ever relied on word of mouth and a few unplanned posts.

What we did

Planned a focused paid campaign on Meta with a single clear offer, designed the creative, and tracked every enquiry it generated.

The result

A measurable spike in footfall through the season — and, for the first time, a clear picture of what each rupee of ad spend returned.

1Clear offer, clear message
100%Enquiries tracked
↑Busiest season yet
Healthcare

Clinic website & identity

The challenge

A clinic had no website and no consistent brand — new patients couldn't find clear information or an easy way to book.

What we did

Designed a calm, trustworthy identity and built a fast, mobile-first site with booking and directions as the obvious next step.

The result

A professional first impression online, and a simple path from search to booked appointment.

1Clear, calm brand
< 2sMobile load time target
↑Easier patient bookings
Fitness

Studio launch content

The challenge

A new fitness studio was opening with no audience and no content to build early interest.

What we did

Created a reel-first content plan for the launch window — a steady stream of short, on-brand videos and posts to build momentum before day one.

The result

An audience that existed before the doors opened, and a content rhythm the studio could keep up after launch.

0→1Audience built pre-launch
ReelsFirst content format
↑Momentum from day one

D2C Product

Product launch funnel

The challenge

A direct-to-consumer product had a good offer but a scattered funnel — ads pointing to a weak page that didn't convert.

What we did

Built the landing page and the ad campaigns as a single system — consistent message, one clear action, conversion tracked end to end.

The result

A funnel where every part points the same direction, and spend that can be judged on real outcomes.

1Unified ad-to-page system
100%Conversion tracked
↑Cleaner cost per lead
